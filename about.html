About

I am a graduate student in the School of Library and Information Studies at Queens College, City University of New York, pursuing a MLS with a certificate in archives, records management, and preservation.

Before enrolling, I was as an instructor and researcher at the Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla, Tehuacán, where I taught courses in history, audio production, and information literacy in the communication and political science departments.

My research interests include archival and cataloging projects relating to music and migration history.
