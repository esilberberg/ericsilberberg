I am a graduate student in the School of Library and Information Science at CUNY Queens College pursuing a Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) with a certificate in archives, records management, and preservation.

Before enrolling, I was as an instructor and researcher at the Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla, Tehuacán, where I taught courses in history, audio production, and research methods in the Communication Sciences department.

My research interests include archival and metadata projects relating to music and migration history.
