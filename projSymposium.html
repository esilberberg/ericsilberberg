Symposium on Cultural Economy

Norteño music is an often derided genre of Mexican music though its historical significance, economic impact and international popularity are undeniable. The Symposium on Cultural Economy brought together musicians, academics and undergraduates at the Benemérita Universidad Autónoma de Puebla-CRS to explore the influence of Norteño music on the identity and economy of the Tehuacán region of Puebla.

The conference featured three events. First, local musicians presented a concert of traditional and contemporary Norteño repertoire. Second, outstanding undergraduates presented original research papers that focused on identity and economic activity within Norteño music. Third, the author and professor Dr. Gabriel Medrano de Luna presented a lecture and workshop on methods for cultural research projects.
