Cantares   Tehuacán

This documentary series, originally presented on 4TV Puebla, tells the story of musicians from the Tehuacán Valley in Puebla, Mexico and how they came to find their musical voice, understand the socioeconomic context of their music, and cope with the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on their livelihoods.
